Southern Baked Mac And Cheese

Description

Southern baked mac and cheese, a staple in all southern homes.

Ingredients

  • 32 oz chicken stock
  • 3 1/2 cups elbow pasta
  • 8 cups of cheese of choice
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 1/2 cup of Heavy Cream
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cups of sour cream
  • Paprika, salt, pepper
  • 1 stick of butter

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil pasta in chicken stock for 7-8 minutes.
  2. Strain the pasta, then mix in 1 stick of butter until it is melted.
  3. Add 3/4 cups sour cream, then add seasonings and mix.
  4. Add in 8 cups of any cheese of your choice.
  5. To create your custard, in a seperate bowl add 3 eggs, 2 cups of whole milk and 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream stir until it is all combined together.
  6. Add your prepared pasta and custard into a cooking pan of your choice.
  7. Bake in the oven at 375 degrees for 45-60 minutes.
  8. Let your mac and cheese rest for 10 minutes, then it is ready to eat!
