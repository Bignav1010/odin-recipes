Lemon Blueberry Bundt Cake

Description

A tasty cake that is great for big outings due to the easy to cut slices. The lemon Blueberry Bundt cake is a delicious and moist combination that is a staple of southern culture.

Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Servings: 16

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Butter Softened
  • 11/2 Cups of Sugar
  • 3 Eggs
  • 1 tbs Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tbs Lemon Zest 1 Lemon
  • 2 1/2 Cups of Flour
  • 1 tsp of Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 1 Cups of Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  • 2 Cups of Fresh or Frozen Blueberries

    • Frosting Ingredients

  • 4oz Cream Cheese Softened
  • 4 tbs Butter Softened
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Powdered Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • White Chocolate Optional

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Spray bundt pan with cooking spray.
  2. Add the butter and sugar in the bowl until light and fluffy. 2 minutes later add eggs one at a time. Then add vanilla extract and lemon zest.
  3. In a seperate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, soda and salt.
  4. Add a tbs of flour to the fresh berries. If you are using frozen bluberries than leave them until unfrozen and ready to add.
  5. Add a 1/3 of flour mixture to the butter mixture, alternating with yogurt, starting and ending with the flour mixture. Mix until just combined and smooth.
  6. Gently fold in blueberries and spoon the batter into the bundt pan, levelling off the surface so that it is even.
  7. Bake for 45-50 minutes until the top is golden brown and toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes.

    9. Frosting Instructions

  9. Mix together all the ingredients, except the white chocolate, until creamy smooth. Smooth over the top of the cooled cake and sprinkle with white chocolate.
